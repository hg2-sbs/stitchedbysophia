The People

Meet the Team

Small but mighty — the duo behind Stitched by Sophia.

Founder & Creative Director

Sophia

A lifelong lover of fashion and craft, Sophia started Stitched by Sophia from a small studio in Old Greenwich C.T., driven by a belief that great style begins with great fit. She trained in fashion design, writes about the industry with a discerning eye, and handles every alteration with the care of a couturier.

When she's not at the machine, you'll find her at a sample sale, deep in a vintage archive, or rewatching old Vogue runway footage.
